HighScore:
Current Points: Main Objectives: Cross the road and collect all the coins without dying.

Frog (Main Character, green) - Use the arrow keys to move the frogs.

Instances (Circles):

  • Car (Dangerous, red) - Avoid touching the car when crossing the road.
  • Plank (Safe, orange) - Jump to the plank platform to cross the river safely.
  • Goose (Safe, white) - Use the Goose to cross the river, but be careful, they will send you backward.
  • Bird (Dangerous, blue) - After collecting all of the coins, the birds will come from different directions to attack you.
  • Coins (Goal, yellow) - Collect them all to get the Final level.
  • Final Dark Coin (Bonus Points, black) - Touch this dark coin to earn more points.

Ground (Rectangle):
  • Grass (Safe) - green
  • Road (Safe) - black
  • River (Dangerous) - lightblue

Keys:
  • Press Arrow to move to the Up
  • Press Arrow to move to the Down
  • Press Left Arrow to move to the Left
  • Press Right Arrow to move to the Right
  • Press Spacebar to Jump
  • Press Enter to Restart
